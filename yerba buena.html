YERBA BUENA

Yerba Buena (Mentha cordifolia) is used to relieve pain (headache, toothache, swollen gums, menstrual pain), cough, colds, and fever. The leaves are used in a decoction by boiling chopped leaves in two glasses of water for 15 minutes, or as a poultice by pounding the leaves and applying them to the affected area.
